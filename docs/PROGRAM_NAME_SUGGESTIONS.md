# ConvoLens - Complete Name Selection Analysis

## Executive Summary

This document provides the comprehensive analysis that led to the selection of **ConvoLens** as the new brand name for the conversation analysis platform (formerly "WhatsSummarize"). The analysis evaluated 18 name options across 8 critical factors to identify the optimal brand identity for market positioning, user appeal, and long-term scalability.

### Decision Summary

**Selected Name:** ConvoLens (Score: 46/50)  
**Decision Date:** December 2025  
**Status:** Implementation Phase

### Previous Brand Analysis

**Former Name:** WhatsSummarize  
**Strengths:** Direct, functional, SEO-friendly  
**Weaknesses:** Limited brandability, lacks sophistication, difficult to verbalize, tied to WhatsApp only

---

## Scoring Methodology

Each name is evaluated across 8 factors (scale: 1-10), with a maximum total score of 80 points converted to a 50-point scale for easier comparison and benchmarking.

**Conversion Formula:** `Final Score = (Total Points / 80) × 50`

For example, a name scoring 74 out of 80 points would receive: `(74 / 80) × 50 = 46.25` (rounded to 46/50)

### Evaluation Factors

1. **Clarity** (1-10): How clearly the name communicates purpose
2. **Memorability** (1-10): Ease of recall and word-of-mouth sharing
3. **Brandability** (1-10): Visual identity potential and marketing appeal
4. **Domain Availability** (1-10): Available domains (.com, .io, .app)
5. **Target Fit** (1-10): Alignment with target audience expectations
6. **Scalability** (1-10): Adaptability for multi-platform expansion
7. **Uniqueness** (1-10): Differentiation from competitors
8. **Professional Appeal** (1-10): Enterprise and B2B credibility

## Competitive Positioning Analysis

### Market Landscape

**Direct Competitors:**

- Slack Analytics Tools (limited, enterprise-focused)
- WhatsApp Business API analytics (basic metrics only)
- Discord analytics bots (gaming-focused)

**Adjacent Competitors:**

- **Buffer/Hootsuite** (Social media analytics) - Professional, dashboard-heavy
- **CoSchedule** (Marketing calendar) - Organized, planning-focused
- **Notion** (Knowledge management) - Flexible, workspace-oriented

### Differentiation Strategy

| Aspect             | Buffer/Hootsuite        | Our Position                  | Advantage         |
| ------------------ | ----------------------- | ----------------------------- | ----------------- |
| **Focus**          | Social media scheduling | Private conversation analysis | Unique niche      |
| **Data Source**    | Public social posts     | Private chat exports          | Privacy-first     |
| **AI Integration** | Basic sentiment         | Deep NLP summaries            | Advanced insights |
| **User Type**      | Marketing teams         | Individuals & teams           | Broader appeal    |
| **Pricing Model**  | Subscription tiers      | Freemium + premium            | Lower barrier     |

### Positioning Statement Options

**For ConvoLens:**  
"For individuals and teams who want to understand their communication patterns, ConvoLens is a conversation analytics platform that provides AI-powered insights from private chat histories, unlike social media tools that focus on public engagement."

**For ChatMeta:**  
"For data-conscious communicators seeking deeper understanding, ChatMeta is a conversation intelligence platform that reveals hidden patterns in your messaging data, unlike basic chat tools that only store messages."

**For TalkTrack:**  
"For anyone drowning in messages, TalkTrack is a conversation tracking platform that surfaces what matters in your chat history, unlike traditional chat apps that lack analytical capabilities."
